About Me

Janet Hwu received her Bachelor's Degree in Mechanical Engineering with a focus on Product Design from Stanford University. She began her career as a Walt Disney Imagineer after becoming a finalist in the ImagiNations design competition, for which she and her partner pitched a restaurant concept based on the Pixar film Ratatouille. Her career as a theme park designer took her abroad to Shanghai, where she oversaw the production and construction of new attractions in Shanghai Disney Resort's Tomorrowland.

Prior to beginning her career in web development, Janet also served as Art Director for the Design Studio at Universal Studios Japan, leading design for new installations in the park. She looks forward to applying her skills and experience to the tech industry.
